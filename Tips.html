Tips for Playing Mafia

Must Know Tips

To succeed in Mafia, whether you’re a Citizen or playing a special role, blending in is critical. If you're Mafia or the Killer, act like a Citizen to avoid drawing attention. Observe how others talk and behave—changes in speech or body language can reveal hidden roles. If accused, stay calm and present a logical defense. Mafia members should quietly spread doubt and steer votes away from their teammates. Detectives should share their findings carefully, not too early to avoid being exposed. Medics should protect likely targets but switch it up to keep enemies guessing

Role-Based Tips

Role Tip
Citizen Stay alert during discussions, gather clues from how others speak, and push for logical voting without being too aggressive
Mafia Act as a Citizen, support votes subtly, and build trust by helping eliminate non-Mafia threats. Never seem too eager to accuse others
Medic Protect players who are under suspicion or valuable to the Citizens, and avoid revealing your identity unless it's critical for survival
Don Stay low-key, act like a regular Citizen, and choose your kills carefully, especially targeting high-impact roles like the Detective
Detective Investigate suspicious players early, and share your discoveries subtly without revealing your role until it’s necessary
Killer Lead the Mafia strategically by guiding who to eliminate, managing suspicion, and protecting your teammates without drawing attention

Hand Gestures

Hand gestures can be an important tool when playing Mafia in person. Here are some gestures you can use to communicate with others in the game:

  • Cross Sign: This sign is used during night for mafia to notify another mafia that he/she wants to open up as fake medic if anything happens. It can also be used during day to tell players that you are a medic
  • OK Sign: During night mafia can’t talk so they have to communicate using sign language, ok sign means detective and using this sign you can tell other mafia that if something happens you want to open up as a fake detective. This way you are wanting other mafias so that no one else opens up as a detective. You can use this sign during day as well if you trust someone to reveal your role in secret
  • Cutting Throat: This sign is used on someone who is suspicious of being a killer. It can be used during a day to notify other players that highlighted player is under suspicion of being a killer
  • Thumbs Down: This means mafia. Gamemaster will notify you if you are detective using this sign in case the person you checked is mafia. You can use this sign during day as well to hint player that you have someone under suspicion of being mafia
  • Thumbs Up: This sign means civilian. Detective will see this sign in case of checking a civilian or killer. This sign can be used during day as well to show someone that you trust them and thing that player is a red card
