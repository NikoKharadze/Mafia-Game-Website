Similar Games to Mafia

If you enjoy Mafia, here are some other social deduction games you can play with friends:

  • Werewolf: Very similar to Mafia, players are divided into werewolves and villagers, with secret roles and night-time killings.
  • Secret Hitler: A hidden identity game where players try to enact policies while others attempt to discover who is Hitler.
  • Resistance: Another deduction game where two teams (Resistance and Spies) try to complete missions without being sabotaged.
  • Coup: A game of bluffing where players use influence cards to eliminate others and become the last player standing.
